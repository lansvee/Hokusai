About exhibition

The exhibition includes a curated selection of Katsushika Hokusai's iconic ukiyo-e masterpieces. From the captivating “Great Wave off Kanagawa” to lesser-known sketches, this showcase explores how Hokusai’s mastery of woodblock printing and storytelling continues to influence global art to this day.

Step into the master's world

The Great Wave off Kanagawa is the centerpiece of Hokusai’s celebrated “Thirty-six Views of Mount Fuji.” This timeless image captures the power of the ocean and the refined elegance of Edo-period printmaking, bridging centuries of art evolution.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to immersing enthusiasts in the world of Hokusai through carefully curated exhibits, lectures, and visual storytelling. By highlighting his boundless creativity and global influence, we hope to connect modern audiences with timeless Japanese art.
